# Phase 4 Preproduction Audit Rules

Phase 4 audits are allowed to be incomplete because validators, Blender reports, and real artifacts do not exist yet.

## Required Inputs for the First Manual Audit

- Character Director brief.
- Character Director task card.
- Reference Librarian report.
- Style Keeper report.
- Anatomy Review report.

## Phase 4 Blocking Conditions

- No brief.
- No task card.
- No reference coverage plan.
- Style family missing.
- Godot target missing.
- Camera context missing.
- Anatomy or deformation risks not recorded.

## Phase 4 Score Guidance

Only score categories with evidence. It is acceptable to mark later production categories as `pending`.

Expected pending categories:

- Topology and edge flow.
- UVs and baking readiness.
- Materials and texture logic beyond initial material intent.
- Deformation readiness beyond risk identification.
- Performance and LODs.
- Godot readiness.
