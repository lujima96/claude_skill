## Purpose

Use this when reviewing concept, proportion, blockout, and LOD stages.

## Readability Tests

- Front silhouette.
- Side silhouette.
- Back silhouette.
- 3/4 silhouette.
- Gameplay-distance camera.
- Small thumbnail.
- Neutral pose.
- Action pose if available.

## What Must Read

- Character role.
- Body mass and stance.
- Head and face direction.
- Weapon or major gear if present.
- Hands and attack silhouette.
- Primary costume shapes.
- Difference between skin, clothing, armor, and hair.

## Common Problems

- All forms have similar size and rhythm.
- Costume details create noise but no readable identity.
- Shoulder and head shapes merge.
- Weapon or gear silhouette is hidden by body mass.
- LOD reduction preserves triangles but loses identity.

## Review Checklist

- Can the asset be identified at expected game camera distance?
- Is the character readable without textures?
- Is the face direction clear?
- Is the action direction clear?
- Do lower-detail views preserve the main read?
